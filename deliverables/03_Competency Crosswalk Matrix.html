Competency Crosswalk Matrix
This matrix demonstrates alignment between the Rhombus University 80-hour Peer Support Specialist Training Curriculum and California Medi-Cal Peer Support Specialist core competencies. The crosswalk ensures instructional coverage across recovery-oriented practice, advocacy, ethics, documentation, crisis awareness, cultural humility, and workforce readiness expectations.
